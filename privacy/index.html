Privacy Policy

SwimFlow is a browser-based tool designed to keep your diagrams private and local whenever possible.

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Data Processing

SwimFlow runs primarily in your browser. Diagram content is generated and rendered client-side.

2. Information We Collect

  • Basic anonymous analytics such as page views and browser type, if analytics are enabled.
  • No account information is required to use core features.
  • No diagram upload is required for standard editing and export workflows.

3. How We Use Information

  • Improve product stability and usability.
  • Measure adoption of major features.
  • Maintain service reliability and prevent abuse.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may use privacy-conscious third-party services for infrastructure, monitoring, or analytics.

5. Cookies

Cookies may be used for basic site functionality and aggregate analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the SwimFlow team through MemoLab support channels.
